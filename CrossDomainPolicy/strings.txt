PLUGIN_XDOMPOL_MODULE_NAME
	EN	Cross-Domain Policy

PLUGIN_XDOMPOL_MODULE_DESC
	EN	Provides a cross-domain policy so Flash or Silverlight apps can interact with SqueezeBox Server.

PLUGIN_XDOMPOL_ALLOWED_DOMAINS_LABEL
	EN	Allowed domains (one per line):

PLUGIN_XDOMPOL_ALLOWED_DOMAINS_DESC
	EN	Each domain is translated into an allow-access-from directive. Enter 'example.com' to only allow access to content from example.com; '*.example.com' to only allow access to content from anything at example.com; '*' to allow access from all content regardless of origin.
